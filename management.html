Top Management

Our top highest level of management in our organization, responsible for setting the the company's directio and goals and making decisisons that heop to ensure that thsoe objectives can be met.

The top of most management teams has at least a Chief Executive Officer (CEO), a Chief Financial Officer (CFO), and a Chief OperationsOfficer (COO).
The exact composition of top managementcan vary depending on the comp;any, but it typcially includes all of the senior-level leaders and managers holding positions of authority and responsibility within an organization.

The main tasks of top-level managers include planning the activities and strategies of the company in general and guiding the direction of the organization. They are responsible for establishing policies, guidelines, and strategic objectives, as well as providing leadership and direction for quality management within the organization. Top management is also responsible for managiing company finances, developing goals and plans to achieve them, overseeing daily operations, hiring, training, and developing talent, establishing and upholding the company culture, and communicating expectations to shareholders.
